Frequently Asked Question about CSED

Everything You Need To Know About CSED

How to join CSED at VIT?

You can join CSED by registering on VTOP during the club/chapter enrollment period, which usually happens at the beginning and end of each academic year.

What types of events and activities does CSED organize?

CSED organizes events to raise awareness on social entrepreneurship, provides insights into real-world problems and solutions, conducts outreach events, and hosts entrepreneur speaker sessions.

How can I participate in CSED events ?

You can participate in CSED events by registering on VTOP under the event registration section. Follow us on Instagram to stay updated on upcoming events.

What are the departments within CSED?

CSED has various departments, including R&D, Marketing, Tech & Design, Events, PR, HR, and Editorial. You can join any department and work to gain valuable skills and experience.

What kind of projects can I work on as a member of CSED?

As a CSED member, you can collaborate with like-minded individuals on projects aimed at solving real-world problems, gaining hands-on experience and making a positive impact.

How can I stay updated on CSED activities and events?

You can stay updated on CSED activities and events by following us on social media and joining our CSED channel on WhatsApp.
